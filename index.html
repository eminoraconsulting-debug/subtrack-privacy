Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Eminora Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Subtrack mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Information: When you sign in using Google or other providers, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture provided by the service via Firebase Authentication.
  • Subscription Data: We store the subscription details you enter (name, price, frequency) securely in Google Cloud Firestore.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device metadata (e.g., OS version, device model) to ensure the App runs correctly.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To synchronize your subscription data across your devices.
  • To send you timely reminders for upcoming payments (via local or push notifications).
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power certain features of the App:

  • Firebase: Used for authentication and database storage (Google Cloud Platform).
  • Google AdMob: May be used to display relevant advertisements. AdMob may collect device-specific identifiers to personalize ads.
  • Google Play Services: Required for Android app functionality and Google Sign-In.

4. Data Retention and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. Your subscription info is stored in encrypted databases in the cloud. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. If you have questions about your information, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
