Delete Your Data

We respect your right to data privacy. You can request the permanent deletion of your Subtrack account and all associated data at any time.

⚠️
This action is permanent and irreversible.

Once your data is deleted, we cannot recover your subscription history, preferences, or account information.

1

Delete In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly from within the Subtrack app:

  • Open the Subtrack app on your Android device.
  • Go to Settings (bottom navigation bar).
  • Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section.
  • Tap "Delete Account" and confirm.

Your data will be wiped from the device and our cloud servers immediately.

2

Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can email us a deletion request. Please include your registered Google email address so we can locate your account.

📧 Send Deletion Request
⏱ What data gets deleted and how long does it take? We will process emailed deletion requests within 48 hours. The following data will be permanently removed: Local subscription data on your device · Cloud-synced subscriptions in our database · Your authentication profile (Google Account link)
